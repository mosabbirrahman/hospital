Frequently Asked Questions

You can schedule appointments three ways: by calling our appointment line at (555) 987-6543, using our online patient portal, or by visiting our clinic in person. New patients should arrive 15 minutes early to complete paperwork.

We accept most major insurance plans including Medicare and Medicaid. Please contact our billing department at (555) 123-4567 ext. 2 to verify your specific coverage. We also offer self-pay options and payment plans.

For prescription refills, please contact your pharmacy first. They will send us the refill request electronically. Allow 48 hours for processing. For controlled substances, you may need to schedule an appointment.

Please bring your insurance card, photo ID, list of current medications, medical records if transferring care, and any relevant test results. New patient forms are available on our website to complete beforehand.

Yes, we offer telehealth visits for established patients with non-emergency conditions. These can be scheduled like regular appointments. You'll receive a secure link to join your virtual visit at your appointment time.
