ABOUT OUR CLINIC

A Legacy of Compassionate Care

Founded in 2005 by Dr. James Wilson, Harmony Medical Center began as a small practice with a big vision: to provide personalized, high-quality healthcare to our community. Today, we've grown into a comprehensive multi-specialty clinic while maintaining our founding principles.

Our 15,000 square foot facility features state-of-the-art examination rooms, an on-site laboratory, advanced diagnostic imaging, and comfortable patient areas designed to reduce stress and promote healing.

OUR JOURNEY

Milestones in Our History

2005 - Foundation

Dr. James Wilson opens Harmony Family Practice with two exam rooms and a vision for patient-centered care.

2010 - First Expansion

Added pediatric services and moved to our current location with 8 exam rooms and an on-site lab.

2015 - Multi-Specialty Center

Became Harmony Medical Center with the addition of cardiology, dermatology, and women's health services.

2020 - Technology Upgrade

Implemented electronic health records system and telemedicine capabilities to enhance patient access.

2023 - Present Day

Now serving over 15,000 patients annually with 30+ providers across 12 specialties in our expanded facility.
